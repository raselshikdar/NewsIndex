Privacy Policy

At NewsIndex, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our website.

1. Nature of This Website

NewsIndex is a news aggregation platform. We do not publish original news articles. We only display publicly available headlines and links and redirect users to the original publishers.

2. Information We Collect

NewsIndex does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or identity.

Any preferences (such as theme mode, favorites, or recently visited sites) are stored locally in your browser using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

NewsIndex does not use tracking cookies. Local storage may be used only to improve user experience (for example: saving theme preference or favorite sites).

When you click a news link, you are redirected to an external website (such as Prothom Alo, BBC, or other publishers).
